# NOTICE

## Project Posture

Vault is a subproject of **Spacecraft Software**, a personal hobby project
maintained by Mohamed Hammad. Its primary purpose is to serve the maintainer's
own use case — a Rust-first Bitwarden client that the maintainer wants to run
on his own machines. Vault is developed at hobby pace and shaped around that
workflow.

A handful of Spacecraft Software subprojects (e.g., **Anvil-SSH**) are
intentionally designed and maintained with general use in mind. Vault is not
one of them at this stage; that may change once it reaches a 1.0 release.
